Our Menu

Chicken

Tender and juicy chicken grilled to perfection. Served with a side of seasonal vegetables and your choice of creamy mashed potatoes or steamed rice. Our special marinade locks in the flavors, making every bite a delightful experience. Whether you prefer it mild or spicy, our chicken will satisfy your cravings and leave you wanting more.

Beef

Indulge in the rich flavors of our succulent beef dishes. From our signature tenderloin steak cooked to your preference, served with a velvety mushroom sauce and accompanied by roasted potatoes, to our slow-cooked braised beef short ribs, falling off the bone and paired with a red wine reduction, each bite is a celebration of taste and texture.

Sushi

Experience the artistry of sushi at its finest. Our skilled sushi chefs meticulously craft each roll using the freshest ingredients sourced from the finest fish markets. From traditional favorites like California rolls and nigiri sushi to innovative creations bursting with flavors and textures, our sushi menu offers a delightful journey for your palate.

Ceola Gourmet - Discover the Taste of Australia

Welcome to Ceola Gourmet, a culinary haven nestled in the heart of Australia. Our restaurant is an exquisite blend of flavors, ambiance, and warm hospitality, offering an unforgettable dining experience that captures the essence of Australian cuisine. From the moment you step through our doors, you'll be transported into a world of gastronomic delights. Ceola Gourmet, named after the fusion of celestial and earthly flavors, represents our commitment to crafting extraordinary dishes that tantalize the senses and elevate the dining experience to new heights. At Ceola Gourmet, we believe in the power of ingredients. Our talented team of chefs, renowned for their culinary expertise and creativity, meticulously curates a menu that celebrates the diversity and richness of Australian produce. We source our ingredients from local farmers and artisans who share our passion for quality and sustainability. This allows us to showcase the very best that Australia has to offer while supporting the local community. Prepare to embark on a culinary journey like no other. Begin your gastronomic adventure with our enticing appetizers, carefully crafted to awaken your taste buds and set the stage for an extraordinary dining experience. Indulge in our delicate Tasmanian smoked salmon, expertly paired with a zesty lemon-infused crème fraîche, or savor the unique blend of flavors in our wild mushroom tartlet, featuring a medley of locally foraged mushrooms nestled in a flaky pastry. As you progress to the main course, be prepared to be captivated by the artistry on your plate. Our menu showcases a harmonious fusion of Australian and international influences, resulting in dishes that are as visually stunning as they are delectable. Immerse yourself in the flavors of our succulent Queensland prawns, delicately grilled and served with a fragrant lemongrass-infused sauce, or savor the tenderness of our slow-cooked Australian lamb shank, braised to perfection and accompanied by a velvety parsnip puree. To complement your meal, our knowledgeable sommeliers have curated an extensive wine list featuring a selection of award-winning Australian wines. From crisp and vibrant whites from the Margaret River region to robust and full-bodied reds from the Barossa Valley, each bottle has been carefully chosen to enhance the flavors of our dishes and provide an unforgettable sensory experience. Indulge your sweet tooth with our heavenly desserts, crafted with the utmost precision and artistry. Dive into our decadent chocolate lava cake, with its molten center oozing with velvety chocolate goodness, or savor the delicate balance of flavors in our lemon myrtle-infused panna cotta, topped with a vibrant passionfruit coulis. Each dessert is a work of art, designed to leave a lasting impression on your palate. Beyond the exceptional cuisine, Ceola Gourmet exudes a sophisticated yet inviting atmosphere. Our contemporary and elegant decor, combined with warm lighting and attentive service, creates an ambiance that is perfect for intimate dinners, special occasions, or business gatherings. Our dedicated staff takes pride in delivering personalized service, ensuring that every guest feels welcomed and cared for throughout their dining experience. Join us at Ceola Gourmet and embark on a culinary journey that celebrates the flavors, diversity, and spirit of Australian cuisine. We invite you to experience the harmonious blend of celestial and earthly flavors that make our restaurant a true gem in the Australian culinary scene. Discover why Ceola Gourmet has become a destination for discerning food lovers and enthusiasts alike.
